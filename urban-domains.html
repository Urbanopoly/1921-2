Urban Domains

Explore a collection of innovative urban initiatives and solutions that are transforming cities and communities.


The Urbanopoly Project

A transformative initiative merging real estate investment with gamified community engagement. Urbanopoly leverages technology to facilitate property acquisitions and redevelopment projects in urban areas, empowering residents to participate in decision-making through interactive features. The project aims to revitalize neglected neighborhoods by turning tax-delinquent properties into community assets, fostering economic growth and improving quality of life.

Urban AI

An advanced analytics division focused on harnessing the power of artificial intelligence for urban innovation. Urban AI provides tailored solutions to municipalities, businesses, and organizations, addressing challenges such as urban planning, redevelopment, blight elimination, and workforce optimization. Using machine learning and predictive modeling, Urban AI delivers actionable insights for smarter, data-driven decision-making in both public and private sectors.

Urban Innovation Exchange (UIX)

A flagship project in Gary, Indiana, UIX is a dynamic hub designed to drive entrepreneurship, innovation, and community engagement. It serves as a collaborative space where startups, creatives, and community leaders can access resources, mentorship, and funding opportunities. UIX fosters cross-sector partnerships and supports pilot programs in areas such as technology, social impact, and urban development to build a thriving ecosystem.

Urban Mobility Lab

A cutting-edge Mobility as a Service (MaaS) platform offering sustainable transportation solutions. Urban Mobility Lab focuses on integrating micromobility options such as e-bikes, scooters, and bike electrification kits. The platform prioritizes job creation through local assembly and maintenance, reduces traffic congestion, and promotes eco-friendly transportation. Urban Mobility Lab partners with communities, corporations, and public institutions to provide innovative commuting and delivery solutions.

Urbanology Solutions

A municipal consultancy specializing in tackling urban challenges through GIS mapping, land management, urban planning, and blight elimination. Urbanology Solutions partners with local governments and organizations to implement data-driven strategies that optimize land use, streamline redevelopment efforts, and enhance community services. Services include forensic property audits, side lot programs, and creating land bank systems for sustainable urban regeneration.

Urban Synergy

An initiative aimed at building a stronger sense of community by connecting resources, businesses, and individuals within urban ecosystems. Urban Synergy creates partnerships between stakeholders to drive economic development, promote collaboration, and cultivate a shared vision for urban revitalization. Programs include networking events, collaborative workshops, and initiatives that support entrepreneurship and resource sharing.

Urban Cash Digital

A fintech platform revolutionizing digital payments for urban communities. Urban Cash Digital empowers local businesses and individuals with secure, convenient payment tools. It facilitates peer-to-peer transactions, mobile wallet integration, and financial literacy programs, promoting greater economic inclusion and enabling businesses to streamline operations while enhancing customer experiences.

Urban Eats Group

A comprehensive network supporting the local restaurant industry through innovative solutions. Urban Eats Group integrates directories, gamified digital coupon campaigns, and eco-friendly delivery services to connect diners with their favorite establishments. By promoting hyper-local dining and offering tools for customer acquisition, Urban Eats Group helps restaurants thrive while creating memorable dining experiences.

Urban Shopping Network

An e-commerce platform designed to amplify urban entrepreneurs' voices and products. The Urban Shopping Network curates unique offerings from local businesses, providing a marketplace for consumers to discover high-quality, innovative products. The platform emphasizes supporting small businesses, driving community reinvestment, and offering exclusive deals to customers.

Urban EYE Media Group

A multimedia powerhouse producing cutting-edge content for urban audiences. Urban EYE Media Group encompasses digital and print publications, interactive magazines, video content, and social media campaigns. Its portfolio includes tools for storytelling, marketing, and education, empowering businesses and communities to share their narratives effectively while engaging audiences with meaningful content.

Urban Podcast Network

A platform dedicated to amplifying urban voices and fostering meaningful conversations. Urban Podcast Network features podcasts on topics like entrepreneurship, culture, social issues, and innovation. By providing a space for diverse perspectives, the network connects audiences with content that informs, inspires, and uplifts urban communities.

Urban Planet Social

A next-generation social networking platform built to strengthen urban communities. Urban Planet Social supports businesses and individuals by fostering connections, promoting events, and enabling collaboration. With features like hyper-local newsfeeds, business directories, and engagement tools, the platform empowers users to build relationships and create opportunities within their local ecosystems.
